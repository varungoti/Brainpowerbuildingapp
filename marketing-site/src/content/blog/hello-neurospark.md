# Hello, NeuroSpark

Welcome. We've spent two years building a brain-builder we wish had existed
when our kids were born. Here's why, what's inside, and what's coming next.

## The five-minute principle

Most parenting apps assume you have an hour. You don't. We assume you have
five minutes between a meltdown and breakfast. NeuroSpark fits the science
into that five minutes — and tracks the rest of the brain so we don't
accidentally over-develop one region while ignoring another.
