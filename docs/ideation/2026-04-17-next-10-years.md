## Grounding (one-screen synthesis)

### Where we are today (codebase)
- **Consumer app**: 0–10 child brain dev, web + Capacitor, 15 brain-region map, 16 AI-age competencies, voice/conversational mode (FSM), parent coaching, weekly intelligence PDF, sibling collab, sensory engine, **30 locales**, portfolio capture, offline packs, premium gating via server entitlement.
- **Adaptive Generation Engine** with per-region weights + per-tag competency weights + exploration bonus + federated v2 (Laplacian noise on deltas, ε=1.0).
- **Studio + n8n + Postiz** distribution layer just shipped (this iteration). 17 image + 18 video providers behind cost-capped routers; brand-safety guard; storyboard human-gated.
- **Production guardrails**: server is source-of-truth for entitlement, conflict resolution rules (UNION / MERGE / LWW) for cloud sync, animation budget ≤40 KB, `prefers-reduced-motion`, hard monthly USD caps.

### Things we have explicitly chosen NOT to do (anti-patterns to keep)
No transfer-claims brain training. No multiple-intelligences / learning-styles framing. No anthropomorphic AI "friend". No universal mindfulness. No strict screen-minute limits (we use AAP 5 Cs). **No developmental claim without a specific citation.**

### Frontier neuroscience (2024–2026) that should reshape the product
- **Glymphatic clearance** is the dominant sleep–cognition mechanism in kids: insufficient sleep mediates ~10.9% of sleep's effect on crystallised IQ + episodic memory in the ~6,800-child ABCD cohort (peer-reviewed). ADHD kids show lower ALPS index linked to language delay.
- **Music training has the cleanest causal EF signal**: meta-analysis k=46, n=3,530, g=.35; preschool fNIRS RCT shows prefrontal efficiency gains. Dose-response: ≥12 wk, ≥3×/wk, 20–40 min.
- **Theory of mind + effortful control at age 6 predict thought/attention problems through age 10** (n=214, *Sci. Reports* 2025).
- **Bilingual EF "advantage" is modality-specific** — strong on working memory + attention, near-zero on inhibition. Don't sell what doesn't transfer.
- **Parent–child neural synchrony** predicts internalising trajectories over 1.5 years (EEG hyperscanning, 2024–25). Validates "co-regulation/serve-and-return" as a paid surface.
- **Deliberate-practice "10,000 hours" deflated**: 1% variance among elites in 2016 sports MA. Drop the framing; lean into varied practice + sleep + rest.

### Regulatory horizon (existential, not optional)
- **COPPA 2.0 effective April 22, 2026** — separate explicit parental consent for **any AI feature processing kids' input**, near-real-time deletion, k-anon/DP-grade de-id, new VPC methods.
- **EU AI Act** classes AI for minors as high-risk; documented risk assessments required.
- **India DPDP Rules** (notified Nov 13, 2025) — VPC via DigiLocker for under-18s.
- **UK AADC** — best-interests-by-default, geolocation off, no nudge patterns.
- **Direction of travel by 2027–30**: on-device inference for kids' AI becomes a de facto requirement; age-assurance + verifiable consent enforceable globally.

### Category landscape (2025–2026)
Khanmigo ($44/yr) + Synthesis Tutor ($119/yr) own the AI-tutor poles. Lovevery added GenAI parent Q&A + Play Finder CV in Nov 2024. Tonies (10M+ Tonieboxes) + Yoto own screen-free audio. Roblox owns 46% of US elementary kids and just published a curriculum-aligned Cube foundation model. Maven + Carrot prove the **employer-benefit channel** for parenting/pediatric tools.

### Skill consensus for 2036 workforce
OECD Future of Skills 2030/2040, EC/OECD/Code.org AI Literacy Framework (May 2025 → 2026 final), MIT RAISE Day of AI, McKinsey MGI Nov 2025 (AI-fluency demand grew 7× in two years). Convergent: **prompt literacy, model evaluation, agent orchestration, AI-augmented reasoning, taste/judgment, focus, embodied cognition, system-thinking, resilience, care/co-regulation**. Maps cleanly onto our 16 competencies.

## Survivors (7 bets, ranked by 10-year leverage)

> Each survivor is a wedge. Most reuse infra we already have; none requires throwing the codebase away.

### 1. The Companion Coach — long-memory, always-there voice partner for the *parent* (not the child)

**Bet**: We win the next 10 years by becoming the always-there thinking partner for parents — not a better activity dispenser. Voice-led, multi-child aware, with 4-week long memory.

**Why now**: Khanmigo + Synthesis are tutor-shaped (child as user). Lovevery's Nov 2024 GenAI parent Q&A validated parent-as-user but is stateless and stage-card driven. Nobody has shipped a parent-facing AI with **continuous memory of *this* child's last month** — and we already have the data (`AppContext`, `ActivityLogs`, `IntelligenceScores`, `weeklyReportData`, `competencyTags`). Parent-child neural synchrony research validates that the lever is the *dyad*, not the child alone.

**Why bold (10y)**: Reframes the product from `app that gives you activities` → `coach that thinks with you about your child`. This is the only positioning a generic LLM cannot trivially replicate, because the moat is *cumulative observational state*. By 2030, parents won't talk to a fresh ChatGPT about their kid; they'll talk to whichever app *remembers* the kid.

**First 4-week move**:
1. Add `coach_memory` table (Postgres, RLS by `child_id`) — append-only "observations" with TTL=180d, content-classifier strip of any non-developmental content.
2. Extend `ConversationButton` + `/coach` Edge with a `context` block: last 4 weekly reports, last 20 activity logs (already in `AppContext`), top-3 weakest competencies. Token-budget the context aggressively (≤2k input).
3. Ship one new behaviour: **rupture-and-repair mode** ("My kid had a meltdown — what do I do in the next 90 seconds?") — guided dyadic breathing + parent script + post-event reframe captured into `coach_memory`.
4. Don't build streaks. Build **weekly insight cards** that quote back what the parent told the coach last week ("Last Tuesday you mentioned bedtime took 45 min — three things changed since…").

**Risks / what could kill it**: GDPR/COPPA risk if memory leaks across families (mitigation: RLS + per-child encryption-at-rest). Parents abandon if the coach feels generic (mitigation: insist on quoting back specifics). Cost (mitigation: long-context only on premium tier; free tier gets stage-card style answers).

**Reuses**: `VoiceSession` FSM, `ConversationButton`, `/coach` endpoint, `parentCoaching` schema, `productAnalytics`, `useEntitlement`.

### 2. NeuroSpark Audio — screen-free companion (Tonies-class device + audio-only PWA mode)

**Bet**: A material chunk of NeuroSpark's growth from 2027–2030 will not happen on the screen at all. Build a **screen-free audio surface** that runs the same AGE — a Tonies-class kid-safe appliance ($79–$149) and an audio-only mode of the existing app for the car / walk / dinner / bedtime.

**Why now**: 10M+ Tonieboxes deployed; Yoto + Toniebox 2 (32GB, Tonieplay interactive games) own the "screen-free audio" wedge. AAP digital-media pressure pushes parents away from on-screen everything. Our biggest objection from parents is "yet another screen". Music training has the cleanest causal EF signal of any intervention in the literature — and music + voice is exactly the audio surface.

**Why bold (10y)**: This is a *new product line*, not a feature. By 2030 every premium parenting brand has an audio appliance — Lovevery, Khan Academy Kids, Bonkers Toys (NeuroSpark's "bonking" cousin). Owning ours means brand-coverage credit accrues *whether the child is on a screen or not*, which is the only honest "screen-time positive" story in the category.

**First 4-week move (audio-only mode first; hardware later)**:
1. Add `audioMode` to `AppView` (additive route, per repo rules) — unified player UI, voice-led navigation, big-tap controls for the car.
2. Reformat **15 highest-coverage activities** into 90–120 s micro-rituals ("toothbrushing while naming categories"; "car ride listing emotions"). Re-use Studio's Kokoro pipeline to generate audio assets.
3. Wire the same `logActivity` so audio-mode plays earn the same brain-region + competency credit as on-screen plays.
4. Phase 2 (8 weeks): publish a Bluetooth + Wi-Fi reference design for a Raspberry-Pi-Zero-2-W appliance (BOM <$30) that any hardware partner can build, running our PWA in PSP mode. Don't try to be a hardware company on day one.

**Risks**: Hardware is a graveyard (mitigation: ship audio-only PWA mode first, validate retention, then partner). NSFW risk on user-uploaded audio (already covered by `safety.ts` Replicate guard). Cost of audio rendering (already absorbed in Kokoro self-hosted infra).

**Reuses**: AGE, `logActivity`, Kokoro voice service, `runAGE`, `OfflinePackButton`, `AppView` additive routing.

### 3. On-device-First AI — turn the regulatory cliff into our biggest moat

**Bet**: Run the parent coach + STT + TTS + safety classifiers **on-device** by default. Cloud is opt-in per session, with a banner. Make the new COPPA 2.0 / EU AI Act / India DPDP / UK AADC requirements not a compliance cost but a *category-defining differentiator*.

**Why now**: COPPA 2.0 hits **April 22, 2026 — five days from this document**. It mandates separate explicit parental consent for any AI feature processing kids' input. EU AI Act adds documented risk assessments. The market clears around on-device by 2027. Khanmigo + Synthesis are cloud-shaped. Lovevery is cloud-shaped. **Nobody has shipped an on-device AI parenting coach yet.**

**Why bold (10y)**: Owning "the only kids' AI app that doesn't send your child's voice to OpenAI" is the easiest premium-pricing story in the category and the only credible answer to a 2027 regulator asking "where does the audio go?". By 2030 cloud-only kids' AI is illegal in 4+ jurisdictions; we're the safe choice for distribution partners (pediatricians, schools, employers).

**First 4-week move**:
1. Bundle a **3 B-parameter on-device LLM** (Phi-3.5-mini or Gemma-3n-2B) via `@xenova/transformers` (web) and `@capacitor-community/llm` (native). Token-budget the same `coach_memory` context.
2. On-device Whisper-tiny.en for STT (already feasible at 39 MB on Capacitor).
3. On-device Kokoro web build (~80 MB) for TTS — already proven in our Studio service.
4. UI: a clear "Cloud / On-device" toggle + a one-tap "show me what you stored about my child" panel (literal DB rows + one-tap purge — also satisfies COPPA 2.0 right-to-delete in near-real-time).
5. Ship a **public verifier** that any third party can run to prove which payloads leave the device.

**Risks**: Quality drop vs GPT-5/Claude (mitigation: hybrid — on-device for routine, cloud opt-in for complex). Bundle size (mitigation: lazy download on first AI use, like Whisper does today). Battery (mitigation: show heat budget; throttle on `Battery.charging === false && level < 0.3`).

**Reuses**: Existing `requireUser` JWT gate, voice safety filter, file-validation primitives, `featureFlags`, server-side `coach` endpoint as fallback path.

### 4. Sleep × Cognition Loop — "Whoop for executive function"

**Bet**: Plug an **objective sleep signal** (Apple Health / Google Fit / Fitbit / Oura) into the AGE so tomorrow's activities auto-adapt to last night's sleep. After a 6 h night, AGE serves shorter, less working-memory-loaded activities and surfaces the bedtime co-regulation routine in `audioMode`.

**Why now**: This is the single best-supported intervention I found in the 2024–2026 grounding. The ABCD-cohort study (n≈6,800, peer-reviewed 2025) shows insufficient sleep raises perivascular-space burden, mediating ~10.9 % of sleep's effect on crystallised IQ + episodic memory. ADHD kids show lower ALPS index linked to language delay. **No competitor in the kids-app space integrates wearable sleep data.** Whoop owns the analogy in adult fitness; nobody owns it for kids.

**Why bold (10y)**: It is the rare intervention with both (a) a clean causal mechanism (glymphatic clearance), (b) an off-the-shelf signal source (every parent has a smartwatch by 2028), and (c) a lever the product can pull *without* the child interacting (parent enables once; AGE adapts forever).

**First 4-week move**:
1. Add `child_sleep_signal` table (UNION conflict-resolution, since sleep nights are append-only).
2. iOS HealthKit + Android Health Connect integration via Capacitor; one-tap parent consent.
3. Extend `runAGE` with a `sleepDebt` modifier: <8 h → reduce `priorityRegions` working-memory weight by 0.85, surface bedtime audio routine on Home.
4. Surface in the weekly intelligence PDF: "Your child slept under 9 h on 4 of 7 nights. Here's the activity pattern that responded best."

**Risks**: Wearables on kids are rare (mitigation: also accept manual parent-logged sleep windows). Privacy theatre risk (mitigation: signal stays on-device; AGE consumes it server-side only as a 4-bucket categorical). Pediatric advice line (mitigation: never suggest a bedtime; only adapt activity load).

**Reuses**: `runAGE`, `adaptiveModel`, `weeklyReportData`, sync conflict rules, `useEntitlement` (gate as premium).

### 5. Open AI-Age Standard — own the protocol, not just the app

**Bet**: Publish the **16-competency framework** (`src/lib/competencies/aiAgeCompetencies.ts`) as `neurospark-ai-age` — an open JSON spec with peer-reviewed citations and reference scoring code. Then build a **verifier badge** that pediatricians, schools, Khanmigo, Lovevery, Synthesis, and Roblox Edu can independently score against.

**Why now**: There is *no consensus standard* for "AI-age skills" in 2026. OECD + EC + Code.org + MIT RAISE + UNESCO have overlapping but incompatible drafts. McKinsey MGI Nov 2025 says AI-fluency demand grew 7× in two years — biggest skill-demand spike in recorded data — but nobody has a measurable rubric for under-10s. **The slot is open and we have the only end-to-end working framework**.

**Why bold (10y)**: This is the "be the protocol" play. Khanmigo can score against our standard; Lovevery can; pediatricians can; OECD can adopt. We become Stripe-of-developmental-measurement, not just an app. Distribution flips: instead of fighting Khanmigo for parent attention, we credentialise them.

**First 4-week move**:
1. Spin off `packages/neurospark-ai-age` (MIT licence, distinct from app GPL/private code) — pure JSON + TypeScript scoring fn + citations.
2. Land a **technical paper** (arXiv + Frontiers in Education) co-authored with one developmental psych academic — adds peer-reviewed weight when we propose adoption.
3. Open `POST /standard/score` Edge endpoint (public, rate-limited, no auth) that any third party can hit with an interaction transcript and get back a coverage delta.
4. Ship `<NeuroSparkVerified />` web badge + JSON-LD that any partner site can embed.

**Risks**: Standards battles are slow (mitigation: don't wait for adoption; ship as marketing differentiator from day 1). Forks (mitigation: MIT licence + governance committee from year 2). Confusion with our paid product (mitigation: spec is free; *measurement-as-a-service* over time is paid).

**Reuses**: Existing `aiAgeCompetencies.ts`, AGE scoring code, citation map already in repo.

### 6. Clinical Distribution Wedge — pediatrician + employer benefit, anchored by a milestone predictor

**Bet**: Don't compete with Khanmigo for parent search ads. Win **B2B2C** through (a) the pediatrician's office and (b) the employer benefit channel — both of which Maven + Carrot have proven for parenting/pediatric tools.

**Why now**:
- Maven (24/7 pediatricians + sleep coaches + parent coaches as employer benefit) and **Carrot × Blueberry Pediatrics 2026** prove employers will pay for pediatric/developmental tools.
- The 9 / 12 / 18 / 24-month well-child visits are anchor moments; a **one-page neurodevelopmental snapshot** generated from our weekly intelligence data is a free leave-behind that drives organic referral.
- We already have the inputs: `competencyScores`, `regionCoverage`, `milestoneChecks`, `parentCoaching` history.

**Why bold (10y)**: Parent CAC via paid social is a treadmill (LTV/CAC compresses every year). Clinical + employer channels have **near-zero marginal CAC** at scale. By 2030, employer parenting benefits are table-stakes; we want to be in the default RFP. Reimbursement-adjacent monetisation (CPT 96112 developmental screening) is on the horizon by 2028.

**First 4-week move**:
1. Build a **milestone predictor**: the existing `MilestoneChecks` schema + `competencyScores` + age curves + small Bayesian model → "your child is on/above/below trajectory for `objectPermanence` (90% CI)". Pre-fill observed behaviour for parent confirmation.
2. Generate a **PDF "well-child snapshot"** auto-mailable to the parent before each pediatric anchor visit (9/12/18/24/36/48 mo).
3. Spin up `partners.neurospark.com` with a **Maven-style integration brief** (SSO via Carrot/Maven SDK, monthly cohort reports, BAA-ready) — even before we have a partner, just to start the conversation.
4. Recruit 5 pediatricians as paid clinical advisors; their feedback shapes the snapshot copy + claim discipline.

**Risks**: HIPAA-adjacent (mitigation: stay developmental, not medical; never suggest a diagnosis). Pediatrician inertia (mitigation: snapshot is the wedge, not the ask). Long enterprise sales cycle (mitigation: parent-driven request from below — "my kid uses this app, can you accept this report?").

**Reuses**: `MilestoneChecks`, `weeklyReportData`, weekly intelligence PDF infrastructure, `aiAgeCompetencies`, audit-logged admin server.

### 7. Coverage-as-a-Protocol — credit-API for Roblox / Minecraft / daycare / siblings

**Bet**: Brain-region coverage shouldn't only count what happens in our app. Open a **`POST /coverage/credit`** API so any third-party experience (Roblox, Minecraft Edu, a daycare provider's iPad app, a grandparent's SMS, a sibling's prompt) can grant coverage credit.

**Why now**: 46 % of US elementary kids are already on Roblox; Minecraft Edu has district contracts in 130+ countries; Tonieplay (Tonies' interactive games) launched 2025. **The child's life-time-on-app is being eaten by experiences we don't run.** Either we credit those experiences and own the *measurement layer*, or we lose the coverage data entirely.

**Why bold (10y)**: This is the second-half of the "be the protocol" play (#5 is the *what to measure*; #7 is the *where measurement comes from*). Combined with the open standard, we become the canonical measurement layer for child cognitive development across any surface. By 2030, daycares and pediatricians ask "what's your NeuroSpark coverage?" the way schools ask about reading levels.

**First 4-week move**:
1. Spec the credit API (signed JWT, idempotency keys, rate limit per partner, 16-competency + 15-region tag taxonomy from the open standard).
2. Build a **reference Roblox plugin** that calls `/coverage/credit` when a kid completes a defined challenge in any Roblox game (Roblox Studio scripting is well-documented).
3. Ship a **daycare provider portal** that lets a single staff log a group activity → auto-attributed credit to enrolled families' children. Single SaaS sale ≈ 30 family installs.
4. Ship a **sibling co-play credit** flow that uses one parent device to log both children, splitting credit by observed engagement.

**Risks**: Credit fraud (mitigation: per-partner caps + anomaly detection in nightly ETL). Privacy (mitigation: partner only sends `anonymous_child_token`; no PII leaves their system). Standards capture by a bigger player (mitigation: ship #5 first to establish goodwill before any commercial gate).

**Reuses**: Already-shipped admin server, existing `requireUser`/JWT pattern, `/admin/postiz/proxy/*` pattern as a model for partner-scoped endpoints, `dbt` mart for analytics.
